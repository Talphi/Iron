A Piece of Pudding.cool
My way of looking at Pudding's way of looking at things.
Overview
Pudding.cool has a habit of telling some of the coolest data stories, covering a variety of domains and categories.

After going through quite a few of them, I was drawn towards the one's that had something to do with music. While most of these datastories fell under the series of 'How Music is Remembered', a few ventured deeper, and explored the structure of music in a quantitative manner.

This one by Collin Morris, not only gave pretty interesting insights regarding how repetitive music lyrics have gotten over the past few decades , but also explained quite a difficult algorithm - 'The Lempel-Ziv' in a manner that most would understand.
